= Fate cards -- Chaos

== Changing of Ways

|===
|Original |New

|*CHANGING OF WAYS*

_Tzeentch, the master of Fates, laughs at the puny attempts to thwart his designs._

Roll a dice for each fate card in your opponent's hand.
On a roll of 5 or 6 the card must be discarded immediately.

*Play this card at the start of the battle, after dealing fate cards.*

|*Changing of Ways*



|===

== Plague Wind/Psychic Blast (1)

|===
|Original |New

|*PLAGUE WIND*

Nominate an enemy unit and roll one dice to hit.
If this hits nominate another unit in the same detachment and roll to hit again.
Continue to nominate units and roll to hit until you roll a miss.
A hit cannot cause critical damage on war engines (even Eldar Titans).

*Play this card at any time.*

|*Plague Wind*

== Warp Frenzy/Psychic Blast (3)

|===
|Original |New

|*WARP FRENZY*

The affected detachment may roll two dice for its close combat score and choose the best.
If the detachment re-rolls its dice, it may re-roll both dice.

*Play this card on a Chaos detachment before rolling the dice to resolve an assault.*
